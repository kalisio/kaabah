    # What is it ?

    Kaabah is a Terraform based solution to build and operate Docker Swarm infrastructures either on Scaleway or on AWS. These infrastructures are bootstrapped with high level services such as traefik, portainer, prometheus and grafana in order to help you operate your cluster easily.

    Sponsored by Kalisio

    Kaabah was initiated by Kalisio to provide us with the necessary tools to manage the different infrastructures we need to support our development.
